Displayscope Privacy Statement

Displayscope (hereinafter referred to as "the App") respects user privacy and is committed to protecting personal information.
The App adheres to the following principles and does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

1. Collection of Personal Information
The App does not collect any personally identifiable information such as name, address, phone number, or email address.

2. Collection of Telemetry and Usage Data
The App does not collect telemetry data such as user activity, usage patterns, or device information except Microsoft Store collected data.

3. Use of Information
The App does not use any information obtained from users.

4. Sharing Information with Third Parties
The App does not share any user-related information with third parties.

5. Consent to Privacy Statement
By using the App, users are deemed to have agreed to this privacy statement.

6. Changes to Privacy Statement
The App may revise this privacy statement as necessary. Any updates will be published on the App repository.
